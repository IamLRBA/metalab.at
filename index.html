Metalab

A space where programmers, engineers, artists, hackers, social scientists, academics, activists, tinkerers, and creative people of all kinds come together to share knowledge and work on their personal and shared projects in our community space.

What Do We Do?

Programming, robotics, DIY, amateur radio, electronic music, analog photography, art installations, visual and other art, upycling, sewing, karaoke, sign language, food hacking.

Members and guests often host events, lectures, and workshops, most of which can be visited without registering. If you are thinking about hosting an event at our space, contact us at: core

What's a Hackspace?

The hacker culture is a subculture of individuals who enjoy the intellectual challenge of creatively overcoming the limitations of software systems or electronic hardware (mostly digital electronics), to achieve novel and clever outcomes.

Hacking is not strictly limited to technology (change?), but can describe a playfulness, cleverness, and exploration in relation to all kinds of activities.

Tools / Infrastructure

We have a large selection of tools and machines, including a laser cutter, 3d-printers, cnc-mill, soldering irons, and oscilloscopes, most of which can also be used by guests.

Some of the tools can only be used under supervision. Best write a mail to @core and ask when someone is available for it.

What Does It Cost?

Many tools and machines are free to use, though we are always thankful for donations for materials and maintance.

Our space is mainly financed through memberships. You are not required to be a member to use the space and host events, but it is encouraged if you visit regularly. Reduced membership fees are available for students and people with low income.

Want to Visit?

If you want to visit the space, we recommend coming on the open day (held on the second Thursday and fourth Friday of each month) where the Metalab is explicitly open to visitors, or the monthly MetaInvasion day, where the space is open exclusively to FINTA* people.

At other times we are open if at least one member is in the space, which is generally the case every day after 6pm.
